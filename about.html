About Us

Video Game 365 has been reviewing games since 2015. Games have evolved much since the 1990s. We provide reviews for games that everyone enjoys to play. Our goal is to try and review as many games as we can and publish these reviews on a variety of platforms. Although we are unable to provide reviews for all games, we will always try our best to provide reviews to games that are most anticipated and games that are nominated.

Video Game 365 also has multiple reviews of the same ogame on different platforms. The reason for this is because not everyone has the same console, or some fans have a problem which platform to buy a particular game on. We do not have the same writers reviewing games, but rather we have multiple writers that review different types of games. We hope to answer all of our fans' questions, but also a lot more than that. We want our fans to come back as often as possible, so we provide the most honest review of video games.
